# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, background, experience level, gender, nationality, or any other characteristic.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experience levels
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other contributors — especially beginners!

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**

- Trolling, insulting, or making fun of others' mistakes
- Public or private harassment
- Dismissing questions as "too basic" or "stupid"
- Any other conduct that would make someone feel unwelcome

## Our Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and will take appropriate action in response to any behavior that violates this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — Issues, Pull Requests, commit messages, and any other communication related to this project.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.

## Remember

> Everyone was a beginner once. Be the mentor you wish you'd had.
